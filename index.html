About Neil

A neighbor who loves music and wants to share it

I'm Neil, a 15-year-old musician from right here in Greenmeadow. I've spent 6 years in school band and 3 years in jazz, and I was honored to be selected for California All-State — one of the most competitive music programs in the state.

Closer to home, I had the privilege of conducting Greenmeadow's own Memorial Day Band — so I know this community, and I care about it. This summer I'm offering lessons to kids and adults who want to discover or deepen their love for music in a fun, low-pressure environment.
